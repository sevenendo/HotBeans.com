Accessibility Specialist

Role Overview

As an Accessibility Specialist, you will ensure all HotBeans websites meet WCAG accessibility standards and provide equal access for all users.

Salary

£28,000 – £40,000 yearly

Weekly Hours

37.5 hours

Requirements

  • Understanding of WCAG 2.1 guidelines
  • Ability to test websites using accessibility tools
  • Knowledge of assistive technologies (screen readers, etc.)

Responsibilities

  • Perform accessibility audits and evaluations
  • Recommend improvements to designers and developers
  • Ensure compliance with web accessibility laws
